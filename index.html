My name is Michael Vega and I am a third year pursuing a BS in Computer Science with a Law, Science, Technology Minor (Pre-law Track). In my free time, I developed LearnASL.org, a gamified language learning app for American Sign Language that utilizes AI to interactively grade users' hand signs through their webcam. This gives a free, fun, and accessible way for casual learners and early ASL learners to pick up sign language.
